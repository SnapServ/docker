RIPE LIR Services - Activation Process

Every ordered RIPE LIR service needs to be prepared according to the customers needs, verified and processed by us and the RIPE NCC. The following steps show the basic process:

Order

Simply start by purchasing our RIPE LIR services offered above and wait for us to get in touch with you.

Preparation

We will guide you through the whole process of preparing your resource request accordingly.

Verification

In addition to a signed contract, we are required to check your identity by e.g. submitting a passport.

Submission

We will allocate your ordered resources and/or submit your completed request to the RIPE NCC.
