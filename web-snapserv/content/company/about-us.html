Our company and goals

SnapServ Mathis is a Swiss company founded in March 2017 by Pascal Mathis focussed on providing various high quality IT services to end users of any size.

Compared to most other companies offering a similar set of products, we are filling a gap in the market by offering premium services without charging our customers with expensive fees. Additionally to our paid offers, we are also offering free services for education and research purposes to assist people in gathering new experiences and participate in various community projects.
